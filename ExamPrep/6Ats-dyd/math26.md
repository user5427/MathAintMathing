26. Tarkime, ξ yra tolygiai intervale [−2; 5] pasiskirstęs atsitiktinis dydis. Kam lygi tikimybe
P (|ξ| > 1)?

P (|ξ| > 1) = P (ξ > 1) + P (ξ < -1)
P (ξ > 1) = 1 - P (ξ < 1) = 1 - (1 - (-2)) / (5 - (-2)) = 1 - 3/7 = 4/7

P (ξ < -1) = (-1 - (-2)) / (5 - (-2)) = 1/7
P (|ξ| > 1) = P (ξ > 1) + P (ξ < -1) = 4/7 + 1/7 = 5/7
